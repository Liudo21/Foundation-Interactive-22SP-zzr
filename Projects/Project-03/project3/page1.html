ARCHIVE OF THE FOOD
I ALWAYS BUY FROM CONVENIENCE STORE

I have many pictures of food in my photo album,


most of which are the small snacks and


fast food I bought at the convenience store.


The 7-11 convenience store on the way to school


is a paradise


and whenever I see those photos


I think back to my high school days.


What is beautiful may not be the food itself


But the memories they carry.
